JEFF BEZOS

The man who starts small but finishes big.


Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos in July 1994,just like many other legendary brands,It had its humble beginnings in Bezos’s garage in Bellevue, Washington. The name itself gives away its speciality of providing a plethora of products, if you stare at the logo for some time you’ll realize an arrow pointing the “A” of Amazon to the “Z” of the spelling in insinuating the clever wordplay showcasing the philosophy of the company that almost anything is available on it.
